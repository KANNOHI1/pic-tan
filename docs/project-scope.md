# Project Scope

## Phase 1 (Now)
- Platform: iOS
- Audience: kids / beginners
- Theme: animals
- Content size: 15 words
- Primary loop: card learning with quick self-assessment

## Out of scope for Phase 1
- Full monetization implementation
- Full spaced repetition optimization
- Multi-language expansion beyond JA/EN baseline data model
- Advanced analytics dashboard

## Phase 2 candidates
- Improved forgetting-curve scheduling
- Randomized tests with filters
- Extended vocabulary packs
- Subscription and one-time pack purchase flow

## Product constraints
- Keep UI fast and simple.
- Preserve unique felt-doll visual direction.
- Keep legal/IP safety in design references.
