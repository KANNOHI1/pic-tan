# Requirements Summary

## Product
- App name: Pic-tan
- Platform: iOS (App Store first)
- Primary users: kids and beginners

## MVP (Phase 1)
- Theme: animals
- Vocabulary count: 15 words
- Study modes:
  - EN -> JA
  - JA -> EN
  - Pictogram -> EN
  - Pictogram -> JA
- Card study loop with self-rating (`perfect`, `ok`, `hard`, `unknown`)
- Basic mascot reaction support

## Differentiation
- Felt-doll style pictogram characters
- Warm diorama-like visual direction
- Strong visual consistency across generated assets

## Deferred
- Full monetization implementation
- Advanced forgetting-curve optimization
- Expanded content packs and test modes
- Multi-language rollout beyond JA/EN baseline

## Note
The legacy Japanese requirements document should be kept as historical source when available.
